About Me

I grew up in St. Louis Park, Minnesota. I went to elementary school at Park Spanish Immersion School, am entirely fluent in Spanish, and graduated from St. Louis Park High School in 2017.

After completing my senior year of high school at Normandale Community College through the PSEO program, I completed another year at Normandale, before beginning the Full-Stack Web Development Bootcamp at the University of Minnesota.

I am an Eagle Scout, have received the Vigil Honor in the Order of the Arrow, and have 3 Continuing Educaiton Credits in Java Programming, and will soon be a web developer.
